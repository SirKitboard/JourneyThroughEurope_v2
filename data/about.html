Journey Through Europe

About :

Journey Through Europe is a travel game of strategy and planning for all the family. It can be played by 2 to 6 players and it contains:
- 1 playing board,
- 6 playing pieces,
- 6 flags in different colors,
- 180 place cards (64 with illustrations and special instructions)
- 6 flight plans, and
- 1 dice

The players go on a journey through Europe. Cards are handed out which determine an everÂ­changing route, different for each player. The idea is to use skill and careful consideration to choose the best route in order to be first home.
